About Yamaha Narayangarh

Welcome to Yamaha Narayangarh, the premier Yamaha dealership in Chitwan. Operated by Navin Auto Centre, we take pride in being the official authorized dealer for Yamaha motorcycles and scooters in the region.

With over 20 years of trusted service, our mission is to provide quality Yamaha products, expert service, and excellent customer support to all riders — from beginners to seasoned enthusiasts.

Our experienced team is dedicated to helping you find the perfect Yamaha vehicle that suits your lifestyle, whether it's for daily commuting, touring, or adventure.

Our Core Values
  • Customer satisfaction with personalized attention
  • Genuine Yamaha spare parts and accessories
  • Expert maintenance and service facilities
  • Reliable and transparent pricing
  • Community engagement and rider safety programs

About Yamaha Nepal

Yamaha Motor Nepal Pvt. Ltd. is a subsidiary of Yamaha Motor Company, Japan — a world leader in two-wheeler manufacturing and technology innovation.

Since entering the Nepalese market, Yamaha has rapidly grown to become the No. 1 bike company in Nepal thanks to its unmatched product quality, fuel efficiency, and cutting-edge designs.

Yamaha bikes and scooters are favored for their powerful performance, durable engines, and low maintenance costs — making them the top choice for millions of riders across Nepal.

Yamaha Nepal’s extensive dealer network, including trusted partners like Yamaha Narayangarh, ensures customers receive the best sales experience and after-sales service in the country.
