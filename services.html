Yamaha Service Schedule & Warranty Policy

Service Schedule

  • First Service: After 1000 km or 1 month (whichever comes first)
  • Second Service: After 5000 km or 6 months
  • Third Service: After 10000 km or 1 year
  • Subsequent Services: Every 5000 km or 6 months thereafter

Warranty Policy Highlights

  • Standard 2 years or 20,000 km warranty for new Yamaha vehicles.
  • Warranty covers manufacturing defects only.
  • Warranty void if service not done at authorized centers or if non-genuine parts are used.
  • Normal wear & tear, consumables, and accident damages are not covered.

Additional Service Benefits

  • Expert technicians and genuine spare parts.
  • 24/7 roadside assistance.
  • Customer support and maintenance advice.
